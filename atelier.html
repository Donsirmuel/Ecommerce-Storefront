Your bespoke journey

Six touchpoints led by the same atelier team from first sketch to final steam.

  1. 01

    Consult

    Share inspiration, silhouettes, and timelines while we note key dates and preferences.

    Mood boards • Measurement capture
  2. 02

    Sketch & select

    Review sketches and fabric swatches, then confirm embellishments, lining, and accessories.

    Digital approvals • Couriered swatches
  3. 03

    Toile fitting

    A first fitting in a toile mock-up so we refine structure before cutting final fabric.

    Two-week checkpoints
  4. 04

    Detail work

    Hand beading, embroidery, and finishing touches completed by our embellishment circle.

    Progress photos on request
  5. 05

    Final fitting

    Final adjustments, bustle rehearsals, and accessory pairing in studio or virtual.

    48hr alteration promise
  6. 06

    Delivery & aftercare

    Your piece is steamed, packed, and delivered with care notes and post-event refresh options.

    Concierge delivery • Post-event cleaning
