Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and returns

Shipping Information

Shipping Rates

  • Within Lagos: ₦2,500 (1-3 business days)
  • Other Nigerian States: ₦2,500 (3-7 business days)
  • Free Shipping: Orders over ₦50,000 within Nigeria
  • International Shipping: Rates calculated at checkout (7-14 business days)

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding public holidays). You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped with tracking information.

Delivery Partners

We work with trusted courier services including DHL, GIG Logistics, and Aramex to ensure your items arrive safely and on time.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to monitor your package's journey to your doorstep.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. Please note:

  • Customs duties and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient
  • Delivery times may vary depending on customs clearance
  • Some items may have shipping restrictions to certain countries

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery.

Eligible Items

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Waist beads
  • Custom or personalized orders
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Gift cards

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@adireandco.com with your order number
  2. Provide the reason for return and any relevant photos if the item is damaged
  3. We'll send you a return authorization number and instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided (return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective)

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll send you an email notification. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors. If you'd like to exchange an item, please contact us following the same process as returns. If there's a price difference, we'll either refund or charge the difference accordingly.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs.

Lost or Stolen Packages

We're not responsible for lost or stolen packages confirmed to be delivered to the address entered for an order. Upon inquiry, we'll confirm delivery to the provided address, date of delivery, and time. We'll also provide the tracking information.

If you believe your package was stolen, please file a claim with the shipping carrier and contact local authorities.
