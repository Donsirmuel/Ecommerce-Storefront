Hero Section

Our Story

Celebrating Nigerian heritage through authentic, handcrafted fashion accessories that honor tradition while embracing contemporary style.

Mission Section

Preserving Heritage, Inspiring Style

Adire & Co was born from a deep love for Nigerian culture and a desire to share its beauty with the world. We work directly with skilled artisans across Nigeria to bring you authentic accessories that tell stories of tradition, craftsmanship, and pride.

Every piece in our collection is carefully curated or handcrafted using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. From the intricate beadwork of coral necklaces to the vibrant patterns of ankara bags, each accessory connects you to the rich tapestry of Nigerian heritage.

Values Section

Our Values

✶

Authentic Craftsmanship

We partner with skilled Nigerian artisans who use traditional techniques to create each piece. Every accessory is a testament to their expertise and dedication to their craft.

◎

Cultural Heritage

Our accessories celebrate the diverse cultures of Nigeria, from Yoruba beadwork to Igbo coral traditions. We honor the stories and meanings behind each design.

☀

Quality Materials

We source only the finest materials - genuine coral beads, authentic aso-oke fabrics, premium ankara prints, and quality brass - ensuring each piece lasts for years.

✹

Fair Trade

We believe in fair compensation for artisans. By working directly with craftspeople, we ensure they receive fair wages and recognition for their work.

☽

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to sustainable sourcing and production methods that respect both people and the environment, ensuring our craft continues for generations.

✧

Community Support

A portion of every sale goes back to supporting artisan communities, funding education, and preserving traditional craft techniques.
