About Adire & Co

Celebrating Nigerian elegance through authentic fashion accessories

Our Story

Adire & Co was born from a passion for Nigerian culture and a desire to share the beauty of traditional fashion accessories with the world. Founded in Lagos, we specialize in curating and creating authentic pieces that honor our rich heritage while embracing contemporary style.

Our name, "Adire," comes from the traditional Yoruba tie-dye technique, symbolizing our commitment to preserving and celebrating Nigerian craftsmanship. Each piece in our collection tells a story of tradition, artistry, and cultural pride.

Our Mission

We believe that fashion is more than just aesthetics—it's a celebration of identity and culture. Our mission is to:

  • Provide authentic, high-quality Nigerian fashion accessories to women worldwide
  • Support local artisans and preserve traditional craftsmanship techniques
  • Bridge the gap between traditional Nigerian fashion and modern style
  • Empower women to express their cultural pride through fashion
  • Create a community that celebrates African beauty and elegance

What We Offer

Authentic Gele & Head Wraps

From traditional aso oke to modern velvet designs, our gele collection features premium fabrics perfect for any occasion.

Handcrafted Jewelry

Coral beads, statement necklaces, and elegant earrings that showcase Nigerian artistry and craftsmanship.

Designer Bags & Clutches

Ankara prints, beaded designs, and leather pieces that blend tradition with contemporary fashion.

Traditional Waist Beads

Colorful, meaningful waist beads that celebrate femininity and African tradition.

Our Commitment

Every product at Adire & Co is carefully selected or crafted with attention to quality, authenticity, and cultural significance. We work directly with skilled artisans across Nigeria, ensuring fair compensation and supporting local communities.

When you shop with us, you're not just purchasing an accessory—you're investing in Nigerian culture, supporting local artisans, and joining a community that celebrates African elegance.
