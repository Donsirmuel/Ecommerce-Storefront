Orders & Payment

Simply browse our collection, click on any product to view details, select your preferred size and quantity, then click "Add to Cart". When you're ready, click the cart icon, review your items, and proceed to checkout.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and mobile money payments through our secure payment gateway powered by Stripe.

Yes! All transactions are processed through Stripe, a secure payment gateway that uses industry-standard encryption. We never store your card details on our servers.

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After this period, orders are processed and cannot be modified.

Shipping & Delivery

Within Lagos: 1-3 business days
Other Nigerian states: 3-7 business days
International shipping: 7-14 business days

Shipping within Nigeria is ₦2,500 for orders under ₦50,000. Orders above ₦50,000 qualify for free shipping. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our courier's website.

Products & Care

All our products are 100% authentic Nigerian fashion accessories. We work directly with skilled artisans and verified suppliers to ensure quality and authenticity.

Most gele fabrics should be dry cleaned or hand washed in cold water. Avoid wringing or twisting. Hang to dry away from direct sunlight. Iron on low heat if needed. Specific care instructions are included with each product.

Store beaded jewelry in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Clean gently with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals. Remove before swimming or bathing.

Yes! We offer custom orders for gele, jewelry, and bags. Contact us through our contact page with your requirements, and we'll work with you to create a unique piece. Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original condition with tags attached. Custom orders and waist beads are non-returnable for hygiene reasons. See our full return policy for details.

Contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with return instructions and a return authorization number. Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Yes! We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors within 14 days of delivery. Contact us to arrange an exchange. If there's a price difference, we'll either refund or charge the difference accordingly.
